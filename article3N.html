Patrick Mahomes inks historic contract extension with Chiefs

Date: June 26, 2025

Kansas City Chiefs quarterback Patrick Mahomes is by far the NFL’s most untouchable player, but it didn’t stop an NFC team from at least trying back in 2022.

The Chiefs altered NFL history forever when they traded up via the Buffalo Bills to take Patrick Mahomes 10th overall in 2017. Fast forward eight years later, and the team has won five conference titles and three Super Bowl rings, with Mahomes claiming two MVP awards (2018 to 2022) as well.

In the 2020 offseason, Mahomes and the Chiefs agreed to a mammoth 10-year contract extension worth $450 million. Here we are in 2025, and there remains zero reason to believe the Chiefs and Mahomes will split over the next decade.

Back in March 2022, John Keim of ESPN reported that the Washington Commanders “reached out to every team to inquire about a quarterback’s availability and the cost,” including the Chiefs with Mahomes.
