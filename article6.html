Odell Beckham Jr. announces retirement

Date: June 27, 2025

Odell Beckham Jr. doesn't have a football team.

That has prompted some corners of NFL social media to start to suggest OBJ is retiring.

Is he?

So far, there have been no substantiated reports of such news. But it does seem like something that could be in the nearer future rather than far.

Odell suited up for the Dolphins in 2024, but he had just nine catches for 55 yards in nine games.

The year before for Baltimore, he had 35 grabs for 565 yards.

OBJ will turn 33 years old in November.
